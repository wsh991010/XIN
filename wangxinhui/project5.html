This is a series of architectural works.

I will use the photos of four photographers to translate into my architectural design.

1. Trapped in the memory sequence

Perform a series of operations with lines, disrupt the original thinking, disrupt reality and memory.

I found the concept of 3 steps. Split, shift and recomposed. And the procession just like memory. In the process of recalling memories, they are fragmented. The remembered fragment may be different from the facts, just like split the process and shift each segment and finally recomposed a new memory process that maybe different from the original one.

ANALYSIS

The photo is the source of my idea. First I split the entire picture and I shift every single segments. Finally composed a new shape that is different from its original shape. Each segment seems to keep shifting to the left because of the filming angle. I create a new picture by the original four parts and increased the sense of conflict.

DIAGRAM

The rule that I decided the cut line is based on the building around the site. There are two temples to the northeast of the site. I think the two temples are important for the design because it’s a place that can gathering people and they can feel the local characteristics. I shift along these cut lines and show the concept on every elevation and plan.

I turned the color glass into several materials like the punching plate and wavy metal surface. So when the light shines into the all material, indoor people can feel the red light go through the punching plates so the shadow will appear circles pattern, and the metal plate can reflect the light which shines on it.

2. Tear The Moment

Within this boundary, we must develop in accordance with reality. Outside the boundary is my imagination space. There is only a line between reality and fantasy.

The concept that I found in the picture is the black area is tear by the white, and the architecture exposed. And I reversed the picture I draw the architecture on the black area the white place left blank.

ANALYSIS

The light arrow insert the darkness just like tear the darkness. I reversed this photo and I draw the darkness part and blank the bright part. There is a clear contrast between light and dark places, just like two different materials. You can clearly see the appearance of the building and the material of the floor in the bright part, but nothing can be seen in the dark place. I want to express the contrast between black and white. The black area is volumes and white area is the path.

The site I choose is treasure hill, there is a special topography which is stepped like stairs. There are mainly three groups of people with the most tourists, and there are many creators stationed there. And there are also very few residences, but they don’t have priva- cy now because there are a lot of tourists near their houses. In the urban atmosphere of Taipei City, Treasure Hill can continue the traditional cultural architecture and retain the cultural local characteristics of the residents. Next to it, the highway is intertwined, forming a cross between ancient and modern.

MODEL DEVELOPMENT

Because of the topography of the site, the design volume forms a spe- cial skyline on the original topog- raphy. Each building has a different shape, plus translucent windows and walls with square windows.

MODEL DEVELOPMENT

I want to create a space that the residence can not be disturbed by tourists and visitors can integrate into local cultural atmosphere. So I made this model, I designed it based on the stair-shape terrain and corresponded the concept the architecture is tear and the road exposed. The wall near the path are transparent or translu- cent like metal plate with holes or wall with small square windows. At night, the indoor lights are the main source of light in the path, so if people walk or stand inside near the window, the shadow will be on the path outside, so there will be many shadows of people and real tourists in the path at night.

3. Loners Among Passers

Fear in uncomfortable situations, feel anxious because of the sight of passers-by, escape from reality, and hide in fantasy.

Passers-by are moving in different directions and speeds, Although they didn't touch him, but some people caught a glimpse of him.

SITE

Linsen North Road is called “條通 ” by residences of Taipei. "條通" means "alley" in Japanese.This area is full of historical atmosphere. It was a high-end residential area built by Japanese decades ago. And now it is full of hotels and bars, and it was once a area that full of people. Although it has gradually declined, many people still come here to eat or hang out, find nightlife every night.

There are not just hotels and bars now, a lot of Japanese and Korean restaurant opened recent year, many people go to have a drink or eat and chat after get off work.

MODEL DEVELOPMENT

I made a sectional model to see the internal space plan and the user's movement trajectory. The yellow part represents the main space where people usually passed by.

When people stand or walk in the yellow part, they can see the different scene in the different height. These spaces are connected from the floor on the first floor to the roof, dividing the overall large space into several small blocks.

MODEL DEVELOPMENT

There are two main roads around the site, one is the road near the building and the other is the alley opposite the building. I use these two roads to determine the configuration and orientation of the main space. The main yellow space seems to come out from the building volume and is bifurcated in two directions.

The program is a solo activities center. Offer many activities which can do by one- self, meet new freinds and make a new rela- tionship here.

PROGRAM

A person who walk on the site that they doesn’t want to contact the eyes with each other, but in this design, people can make a romance relationship with strangers in whatever they want, like some private dark places, a open transparent cafe or a plat- form where you can watch passersby.

The program is a solo activities center. Offer many activities which can do by one- self, meet new freinds and make a new rela- tionship here.

4. Dis - Perse

History and modernity switch between time and space. Feel like I’ve been lost in time.Disperse in the reality but not a person in the fantacy world.

The sunshine is thick and thin, and its brightness is also different, just like different lights shining into the water, but in fact there is only one light source.

SITE

My base was chosen at the Huwei Fishing Port in Tamsui. This place is located at Hongmao City to the north and Danshui River to the south. It has a strong historical texture, so I analyzed the materials and construction methods of Hongmao City. I combined red brick buildings with historical textures and modernist concrete materials. In order to contin- ue the local architectural features, I used round arch design elements. Although it is a round arch, it feels completely different. Two different materials and construction methods are like a concrete building built on a red brick building. The shape of the red brick building is relatively regu- lar, and the concrete building is softer, and you can see some curvature. Take a window view of a round arch, you can see that different materials are stacked on top of each other, there are water, red bricks, concrete, different forms of glass and bricks arranged to open the hole.

Tamsui is a small town full of history. It was the first port to be developed in northern Taiwan. It opened for trade in 1858. Today's Tamsui District is formerly known as "Huwei".

DIAGRAM

The modern concrete structure is placed on the historical red brick building, showing a sense of conflict caused by different materials. The shape of the red brick volume is formed by arranging cubes, and the concrete struc- ture is a soft curve. Although the appearance is completely different, the inte- rior is connected by moving lines and spaces.

The bulky red bricks are combined with light concrete columns, allowing users to have a completely different experience.

MODEL

Dis-perse has two meanings, one means separation, and the other is what I interpreted. Perse means person. It means not a person, but a space where people gather.

MODEL

Interior lights illuminate the texture of red brick and concrete from arched windows. Forget that they are in the present or the past.

Light streams from arched win- dows and red brick crevices, illuminating the historic semi-outdoor walk. The river water reflects the lights, and the boat enters the red brick atmosphere after passing through the concrete bridge, as if traveling through time and space.
